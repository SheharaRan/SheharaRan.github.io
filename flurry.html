Flurry

Marketing

Flurry is one of NYU's Inter-Residence Hall Council's marquee events. IRHC rents out Central Park's Wollman Rink and over 3,000 residents attend! As the VP of Communications on IRHC, I created the marketing materials which include a poster, a two-side postcard, and a sticker. I wanted to capture the fun atmosphere of Flurry while staying true to its winteresque theme!
