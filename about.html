Hey! I'm Shehara Ranasinghe, a NYC based graphic designer specializing in digital and print media, branding and identity, and web design. My passion for design grew from a need to create aesthetically pleasing and meaningful designs for brands, companies, and users. I am inspired by music, life experiences, and the amazing city around me. My design process is best described as human-centered, research-intensive, and interactive. When I am not playing around on Illustrator I either attending class at NYU's Tandon School of Engineering as a sophomore studying Integrated Digital Media, listening to music at the Brooklyn Promenade, or exploring the city.
